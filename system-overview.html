How It Works

Your cleaning automation runs 24/7 in the background. It watches your Airbnb calendar, automatically notifies your cleaner when a guest checks out, and keeps you updated on everything — without you lifting a finger.

1

📅 Calendar Monitoring

The system checks your Airbnb calendar every 30 minutes for upcoming checkouts. When a guest is checking out today or tomorrow, it automatically triggers the next step.

2

📲 Cleaner Gets Notified

Your cleaner receives a Telegram message with the property address, how long the guest stayed, and any special instructions. It's personalized and clear — no confusion.

3

💬 Two-Way Communication

Your cleaner replies directly in Telegram. The system understands their response automatically — whether they're done cleaning, reporting an issue, or asking a question.

4

🔔 Smart Routing

If the cleaner reports a maintenance issue or has a question, you get notified immediately. If they just say "done," it's logged automatically and you don't get bothered.

5

📊 Everything Tracked

Every booking, every dispatch, and every cleaner response is logged to a Google Sheet you can access anytime. Full history, full visibility, zero manual data entry.

Getting Started

Setup is simple. Here's everything we need from you to go live:

1

Share Your Airbnb Calendar Link

Go to your Airbnb hosting dashboard → Calendar → Export Calendar. Copy the iCal link and send it to us. That's how we see your bookings.

2

Your Cleaner Downloads Telegram

Telegram is a free messaging app (like WhatsApp). Your cleaner downloads it, and we connect them to the bot. Takes 2 minutes.

3

Tell Us Your Property Details

Property address, cleaner's name, and any standing instructions (e.g., "always check patio furniture"). We configure it all for you.

4

We Flip the Switch

Once everything is configured, the system goes live. Your next checkout will be handled automatically. You just sit back and watch it work.

🚀 No software to install. No apps to learn. No setup fee.
Everything runs in the cloud. You interact through Telegram — an app you already know how to use.
